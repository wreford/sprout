Sport vs Conservation Licence:
Ontario offers two licence types. The Sport licence has higher catch limits. The Conservation licence has reduced limits and is lower cost. Limits shown as S / C (e.g., "4 S / 2 C" means 4 for Sport, 2 for Conservation).
