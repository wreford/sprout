Full Monte Carlo | Atomic Constraint Engine

What if every decision on a nuclear mega-project could show you its probabilistic future?

NDX 5x1000 MWe $58B budget 108 months 11,000 peak crew 3,000+ atoms

This demo builds a fictional 5-unit nuclear station from first concrete to commercial operation. The entire project -- schedule, cost, risk, workforce, field data -- is modelled as a single atomic constraint graph.

Work follows Advanced Work Packaging (CWA / CWP / IWP) with PMI earned value, CSA N286 nuclear quality, and CNSC regulatory hold points. Every atom's completion is derived from its dependencies, never stored as a flag.

The Monte Carlo engine runs 1,000+ iterations against 16 risks and 4 opportunities to produce P10/P50/P80/P90 forecasts for both schedule and cost. Resolve a triage, fire a risk, capture a field observation -- the probability distribution re-derives in real time.
