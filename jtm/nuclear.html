ACE
Atomic Constraint Engine

ACE models every project artifact as a constraint-bearing atom and continuously resolves schedule, cost, risk, contracts, procedures, systems, and field execution from a single source of truth.

A proof-of-concept for nuclear construction project controls. One model drives everything: the schedule, cost, risk, constraints, and 3D site — all derived from the same atomic data.

NDX 5x1000 MWe — a fictional 5-unit station combining CANDU, AP1000, and EPR design elements. $58B total installed cost. 108-month construction schedule. 11,000 peak workforce.

Every work package, constraint, risk, and procedure is an atom — a typed node with relationships. Completion flows through the graph: gates wait on dependencies, rollups aggregate children, procedures raise constraints. The Monte Carlo engine runs 1000 iterations against the risk register to produce P10/P50/P80/P90 forecasts.

Nothing is hardcoded. Change a dimension, resolve a triage, fire a risk — the entire model re-derives.

Built with JTM (JavaScript Text Media) and ACE (Atomic Constraint Engine).
