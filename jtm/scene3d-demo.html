JTM -- THE WHOLE VIDEO (EDIT ME)

Try it: change a camera pos, retime a key in timeline, recolor the beam, or add an object like {"id":"tank","shape":"cyl","pos":[-5,1,3],"size":[2,2],"color":"#3E78C9"}. Apply, and the shot changes. A diff of this text is a diff of the film.

Runs locally, no libraries. The LLM's role here is author, not tracer: it writes this text directly, renders, critiques, revises -- coordinates are world-space and language-shaped, projection is the decoder's problem.
